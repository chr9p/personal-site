About me and my crew

As previously stated, my name is Spike Spiegel. I was born in the year 2044 and I am originally from Mars. You could call me a cowboy, in other words a bounty hunter. What I basically do is to hunt down criminals and turn them in to the police for money. I am not alone though. With me I have my companions whom I work together with. We are a diverse group of people containing: Me, Jet, Faye, our dog Ein, and our superhacker Edward. My motto is to just go with the flow and "whatever happens, happens..."
